Project Overview

Al-Barakah Group office in Gulshan 02, Dhaka (2800 sq. ft.) features a modern, high-contrast interior design. Key elements include a dramatic matte black ceiling contrasting with crisp white walls and warm wood flooring. The design utilizes a sophisticated color palette of black, white, and a vibrant royal blue and teal with gold accents.

  • Executive Cabin: Showcases a large black desk against a stunning blue marble-effect feature wall and luxury teal velvet seating.

  • Open Office: Features white modular desks with bright blue privacy screens and ergonomic blue chairs, maximizing storage with seamless white cabinetry.

  • Waiting Area: Welcomes visitors with the backlit company logo, soft beige seating, and a striking vertical green garden wall for a biophilic touch.


The overall look is highly professional, efficient, and aesthetically polished, using sleek linear lighting to enhance the contemporary atmosphere.

Design Concept

The Al-Barakah Group office in Gulshan 02 spans 2,800 sq. ft., crafted as a bold expression of modern sophistication. The concept revolves around high-contrast elegance—where a dramatic matte black ceiling meets crisp white walls and warm wood flooring to create depth, balance, and visual impact.

A refined palette of black and white is elevated with vibrant royal blue, rich teal, and subtle gold accents, establishing a dynamic yet professional atmosphere. Each space is thoughtfully designed to reflect authority, efficiency, and contemporary luxury.

The Executive Cabin makes a powerful statement with a sleek black desk set against a striking blue marble-effect feature wall, complemented by plush teal velvet seating—an environment that embodies leadership and confidence.

The Open Office is designed for productivity and clarity, featuring white modular workstations with bright blue privacy panels, ergonomic seating, and seamless integrated storage to maintain a clean, organized workflow.

The Waiting Area offers a warm yet impressive welcome, highlighted by a backlit company logo, soft beige seating, and a vertical green garden wall that introduces a calming biophilic element.

Sleek linear lighting enhances the architectural lines throughout the space, reinforcing a modern, highly polished, and professional ambiance.


Conclusion

The Al-Barakah Group office in Gulshan 02 stands as a refined balance of bold aesthetics and functional efficiency. Through its high-contrast palette, premium materials, and thoughtfully planned spatial flow, the design creates an environment that reflects confidence, professionalism, and forward-thinking leadership. Every element—from the striking executive cabin to the dynamic open workspace and welcoming reception—works cohesively to deliver a modern corporate identity that is both visually impactful and purpose-driven.
