Project Overview

The interior design of the "Salman Residence" is a prime example of modern luxury residential design. The project successfully combines a sophisticated, neutral color palette with bold accents, high-end materials, and elegant detailing to create a timeless and opulent living space. The design focuses on clean lines, symmetry, and strategic use of lighting to enhance the feeling of grandeur and comfort.

Design Concept

Color Palette: The design is built on a foundation of neutral tones, primarily cream, beige, and white, which creates a serene and spacious feel. This calm backdrop is punctuated with luxurious touches of gold and brass, along with the bold use of deep blue and black. The combination of light walls and dark, statement furniture provides a stunning visual contrast.

Walls and Paneling: A key feature throughout the residence is the use of wainscoting and wall molding. This classic architectural detail is updated with a modern twist, painted in a neutral, glossy finish. In the living room and hallway, these panels are a uniform cream, providing a textured and elegant backdrop. In the family living or a common area, a bold, large-format marble wall with striking black and white veining acts as a dramatic focal point, showcasing the project's high-end material selection.

Flooring: The flooring varies to define different functional zones.

Common Areas: The hallways and main living spaces feature a spectacular geometric patterned marble floor in black and white. This bold pattern not only adds a high-end, classic feel but also creates a sense of direction and visual rhythm, guiding movement through the space.

Living/Dining: The living and dining areas feature light, polished marble or tile flooring. The family living area seems to have warm, polished wood flooring with a curved detail, which adds warmth and defines the more intimate space.

Lighting: Lighting is used both functionally and as a key decorative element.

Ceilings: Recessed LED strip lighting is used to create a subtle glow within the ceiling coves, highlighting the gold and brass accents.

Conclusion

The "Salman Residence" project in Gulshan 01 is a showcase of luxurious and meticulous design, where every detail, from the selection of materials to the placement of lighting, contributes to an overall sense of refined elegance and a comfortable, modern lifestyle.

The interior design of "Shomrat Residence" is a sophisticated and modern project that exudes an air of luxury and refined elegance.
